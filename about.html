About PlayFutsal

PlayFutsal is a web page to promote Futsal in Nepal, which contains all the information about the futsals situated in the Kathmandu Valley. It provides the players with necessary information about their desirable futsals in the Kathmandu valley. It provides them basic information such as address, telephone number, timing and prices of the futsals. Futsal is becoming an appropriate choice and an alternative way of fun for many people. The craze for futsal is increasing in the country lately, with people of any age, be them children, youths and elderly, getting attracted to the sport. So, PlayFutsal keeps the players updated by providing them with necessary information. It also provides goodwill through physical fitness and promotes a productive outlet in our society.
